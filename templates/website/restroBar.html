Restaurant & Bar

The pleasure of authentic flavors

Eat and drink very well. Simple, isn't it? It is our promise and also our business card. At Royal Hotel the chef and the kitchen staff are a strong point, the wine list is the result of real selection, cocktails and beverages have class to sell.





THE WAY OF TASTE

Raw materials, combinations, creativity, refinements, abundance.
The Royal Hotel at dinner, history of unrivaled excellence.

The kitchen has a long history and a wide geography, both of which span traditions , fantasies and even experiments .

In our restaurant the ingredients are natural, the wise procedures, the healthy result.

The Belvedere menu responds to a precise philosophy:

"Everything starts from freshness , and freshness has to come back".

But with many intermediate stages, those that allow creativity to transform simple

and natural ingredients into elaborate dishes .


In short, our strength is to integrate the gastronomic criterion, therefore the pleasure of food , with that of health, a mix

between emphasis on taste and quality choices. Every evening at the Belvedere a ritual is staged: guests who pass

admiringly along the buffets to see "what is today". And today, that is, always, there are five meters of elaborate

appetizers

including vegetables au gratin , crudités , costaceans , mushrooms , cheeses , focaccia and special breads, smoked

and non-smoked meats …

While the central console offers at least two first courses of the great Italian and Indian tradition ,

the best second courses of fish and meat , crispy or seasoned side dishes.

To all this must be added the strictly homemade pastry , with at least three specially prepared desserts of the day.

And lastly a sumptuous offer of fresh seasonal fruit

A BAR FOR FRIEND

A coffee at all hours, a glass of great wine and ...

The Belvedere has an internal bar, able to offer its guests all types of cafes , juices , herbal teas

and long drinks throughout the day, but also fine wines and spirits of great history in the after dinner .

Located in one of the most beautiful rooms, in front of a window that opens onto the swimming pool,

our bar serves internal and external tables.

Here too, the watchword is quality : in the choice of coffee blends, or in the types of whiskey and cognac

and in everything necessary to emphasize a moment of pleasant sipping.

Next to the bar there is a computer available for guests for all types of navigation.
